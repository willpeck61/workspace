Approved products for approved applicators

Finnish Coatings is a specialist paint supplier for the industrial sector.  Our main objective is to work closely with our customers to ensure the application of the products we provide is right first time.

The experience within our team allows us to understand the requirements and challenges involved within the various sectors.

Our product portfolio is predominantly waterborne and we have the capabilities to source or manufacture bespoke products and finishes.

We operate using a seven stage process from establishing our customers needs and continuing the journey right through to project completion.

The importance of correct and diligent application of our products is crucial therefore we have a network of approved paint applicators who have been certified via our stringent vetting process.

We strongly believe this process enables us to match the right team to support our customers projects.

Applicator Check

Finnish Coatings applicators are vetted before they are displayed on our website.  We believe our vetting process enables our customers to feel confident that the recommended product is applied as the manufacture intended.   This process also insures that our approved applicators work in line with both government legislation and current laws.  Our approved applicators understand the importance of Finnish Coatings relationship and transparency with its clients and will adhere to its principles of working, namely:

  • >  Health & Safety
  • >  Honesty and integrity
  • >  Manage client expectations
  • >  Adherence to Finnish Coatings reporting processes
  • >  Client feedback

Finnish Coatings customers will have the opportunity to give feedback at any point of the project via our reporting processes.

Watertight Roof Waterproof System

Description

A seamless roofing system based on moisture triggered polyurethane technology. The system consists of a chopped strand reinforced base layer, sealed with a finish coat.

Uses

  • >  Roofs
  • >  Balconies
  • >  Water Towers

Benefits

  • >  Seamless - no joints to leak.
  • >  Excellent Waterproofing.
  • >  Range of colours.
  • >  UV Resistant.
  • >  No embrittlement with age.
  • >  Bonds to many existing roof structures.
  • >  Fire rated to BS 476 part 3 EXT F AA and EN 1187 B Roof (t4).

Train Exterior

  • Client: The Rail Sector
  • Date: 2011 - Present
  • Service: Industrial Paints and Application

One of our customers has been operating a successful paint spraying business within the rail sector for the past 22 years and approached Finnish for advice regarding a change of paint manufacture with the goal to improve productivity, quality of finish as well as reducing cost.

Given the safety and compliance processes within this particular industry our customer required support form Finnish with the relevant parties for the approval and certification of this new product line.  Working together and ensuring the correct processes had been taken our customer is now using a superior product which has enabled them to achieve their goals.
