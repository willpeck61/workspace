ABOUT US

Finnish Coatings is a specialist paint supplier for the industrial sector.  Our main objective is to work closely with our customers to ensure the application of the products we provide is right first time.

The experience within our team allows us to understand the requirements and challenges involved within the industrial sector.   Our product portfolio is predominantly waterborne and we have the capabilities to source or manufacture bespoke products and finishes.

We operate using a seven stage process from establishing our customers needs and continuing the journey right through to project completion.  The importance of correct and diligent application of our products is crucial therefore we have a network of approved paint applicators who have been certified via our stringent vetting process.

We strongly believe this process enables us to match the right team to support our customers projects.
